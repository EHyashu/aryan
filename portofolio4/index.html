About Me

Hey, I’m Aryan Khatri — someone who believes that technology isn’t just about code; it’s about creating impact. My journey began with curiosity — how does AI think, how does data tell stories, and how can machines make smarter decisions than we do? That curiosity soon turned into a purpose.

Today, I’m pursuing my B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, building projects that bring ideas to life — from AI interviewers that help students, job seeker and company to fraud detection systems that protect digital payments. I enjoy crafting end-to-end solutions where creativity meets logic — using tools like FastAPI, Docker, MLflow, and Streamlit to make AI systems production-ready.

But my vision goes beyond projects. I dream of building tech-driven businesses — blending AI, blockchain, and IoT — to solve real-world problems and create transparent, intelligent ecosystems.

Outside the screen, I’m either in the gym, exploring new places, or reflecting on how to become 1% better every day. For me, progress isn’t just about learning more — it’s about building something that lasts.
